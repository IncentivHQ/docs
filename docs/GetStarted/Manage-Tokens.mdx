## Manage Tokens on the Portal

The **Manage Tokens** feature allows you to control which assets appear in your wallet within the Incentiv Portal. From the **My Assets** section, you can open **Manage Tokens** to view your current tokens or import additional assets to display in your wallet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Log in to the Portal">
    ![Managetokens1](/images/Managetokens1.png)

    1. Open **portal.incentiv.io** in your browser. 2. Log in to your Incentiv account using **MetaMask** or **Passkey**. 3. Navigate to the **Manage Assets** section of the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Open Manage Tokens">
    ![Managetokens2](/images/Managetokens2.png)

    1. Click the **Manage Tokens** button to open the panel.
    2. View all tokens currently enabled in your wallet under the **My Assets** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Import or Enable Tokens">
    ![Managetokens3](/images/Managetokens3.png)

    1. Switch to the **Import Assets** tab.
    2. Browse the list of available tokens.
    3. Use the toggle next to a token to **turn it on or off**.
    4. Enabled tokens will appear in your **My Assets** wallet view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Turning a token on or off only affects its visibility in your portal wallet. It does not move or transfer any assets on-chain.
</Tip>
