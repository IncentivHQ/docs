Welcome to **Incentiv Mobile Onboarding**.

This guide walks you through connecting to Incentiv using either **MetaMask Mobile** or **Passkey Smart Wallet**.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Connect using MetaMask">
    Make sure you have the **MetaMask mobile app** installed before starting.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Add Incentiv as a Custom Network">
        1. Open your **MetaMask mobile app**.
        2. Tap the **network selector** at the top.
        3. Tap **Add Network**.
        4. Choose **Add a custom network**.
        5. Fill in the required details:
           - Network Name
           - RPC URL
           - Chain ID
           - Symbol
           - Block Explorer URL
        6. Tap **Save**.

        ![Mobile Onboarding1](/images/MobileOnboarding1.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Open the Incentiv Portal Inside MetaMask">
        Your MetaMask wallet is now configured to interact with Incentiv.

        1. Inside the MetaMask app, tap the **browser icon**.
        2. In the search bar, enter: **portal.incentiv.io**
        3. Open the Incentiv Portal within the MetaMask in-app browser.

        ![Mobile Onboarding2](/images/MobileOnboarding2.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Start Onboarding">
        1. On the welcome screen, tap **MetaMask**.
        2. Enter your preferred **display name**, or tap **“Decide for me”**.
        3. Tap **Continue**.

        ![Mobile Onboarding3](/images/MobileOnboarding3.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 4: Approve the Connection">
        1. MetaMask will prompt you to approve the connection request.
        2. Review the details carefully.
        3. Tap **Approve**.

        ![Mobile Onboarding4](/images/MobileOnboarding4.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 5: You're All Set">
        Your MetaMask wallet is now connected to Incentiv.

        You can explore the ecosystem, complete transactions, and start earning rewards.

        ![Mobile Onboarding5](/images/MobileOnboarding5.png)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Tip>
      For the smoothest experience, always access the Incentiv Portal from inside the MetaMask in-app browser after switching to the Incentiv network.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Connect using Passkey">
    Create a secure, passwordless **Incentiv Smart Wallet** using your device’s built-in biometric authentication.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Open the Portal">
        1. Open your mobile browser.
        2. Go to **portal.incentiv.io**.
        3. On the welcome screen, select **Passkey**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Choose a Name">
        1. Enter your preferred **display name**.
        2. Or tap **“Decide for me”** to auto-generate one.
        3. Tap **Continue**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Verify with Biometrics">
        1. Tap **“Verify my ID”**.
        2. Confirm using **Face ID**, **Fingerprint**, or your device PIN.
        3. Your Smart Wallet will be created instantly.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 4: You're All Set">
        Your Incentiv Smart Wallet is now active and securely tied to your device.

        You can now participate in activities and earn rewards with one tap.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Tip>
      You just earned a Badge for activating your first Smart Wallet on Incentiv.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
