# Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Start Migration from Testnet v1">
    When you log in to the Incentiv Testnet Portal, [testnet.incentiv.net](http://testnet.incentiv.net), a non-dismissible popup will appear:  **“Incentiv Testnet v2 Is Live”**\
    • Click **Migrate to Testnet v2** to proceed.

    ![Screenshot 2025-09-08 at 9.28.23 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-09-08at9.28.23PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Create Your New Wallet">
    You’ll be taken to the new testnet, [testnet.incentiv.io](http://testnet.incentiv.io), and see: **“Welcome to Incentiv Testnet v2”**\
    • Click to **Create to your new wallet**.

    ![Screenshot 2025-09-08 at 9.28.56 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-09-08at9.28.56PM.png)

    <Tip>
      Need help in creating your wallet? See this [Registration tutorial.](https://docs.incentiv.io/docs/GetStarted/Incentiv_Registration)
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Request Migration">
    On the Incentiv Testnet v2, you will be prompted to request migration and the system will move you back to Testnet v1\
    • Click to **Request Migration**.

    ![Screenshot 2025-09-08 at 9.29.13 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-09-08at9.29.13PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Migrate Your Assets and Badges">
    After creating your wallet, on Testnet v1, you’ll be prompted with:  **“Migrate to Incentiv Testnet V2”**\
    • Click **Start Migration** to continue.

    ![Screenshot 2025-09-08 at 9.29.25 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-09-08at9.29.25PM.png)

    <Info>
       This may take a few minutes to a few hours. Please be patient.    
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5: Your Migration is Complete">
    After the process is done, you will see the message \*\*“Your migration is complete“ \*\*\
    • Click **Navigate to Testnet v2** to start your journey

    ![Screenshot 2025-09-08 at 9.29.40 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-09-08at9.29.40PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6: Final Step – Welcome to Testnet v2">
    Once migration is complete, you’ll be automatically redirected to the new testnet.\
    A welcome message will appear: **“Welcome to incentiv Testnet”**

    ![Screenshot 2025-09-08 at 9.31.02 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-09-08at9.31.02PM.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>Congratulations! You have been awarded the Migration Badge</Check>
