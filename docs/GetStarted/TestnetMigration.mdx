# Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Start Migration from Testnet v1">
    When you log in to the Incentiv Testnet Portal, a non-dismissible popup will appear:  **“Incentiv Testnet v2 Is Now Live”**\
    _“Let’s migrate your account and assets to the new testnet.”_\
    • Click **Create Account on Testnet v2** to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Create Your New Wallet">
    You’ll be taken to the new testnet and see: **“Welcome to Incentiv Testnet v2”**\
    • Click to **create your new wallet**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Migrate Your Assets and Badges">
    After creating your wallet, you’ll be prompted with:  **“Let’s migrate your assets and badges”**\
    • Click **Start Migration** to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Confirm Migration from Testnet v1">
    You’ll be redirected back to the legacy testnet to complete the process.\
    You’ll see: _“Click below to move your assets and badges to Incentiv Testnet v2.”_\
    _“This may take a few minutes. Please be patient.”_\
    • Click **Start Migration** again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5: Final Step – Welcome to Testnet v2">
    Once migration is complete, you’ll be automatically redirected to the new testnet.\
    A welcome message will appear: **“Welcome\! Your account, tokens, and badges will be live soon on Incentiv Testnet v2. Enjoy your experience.”**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  Congratulations\! You've been awarded with the "Testnet Pioneer" badge.
</Check>
