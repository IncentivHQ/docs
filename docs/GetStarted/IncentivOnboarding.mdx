## Overview

Incentiv offers multiple ways to get started depending on your preferred setup:

- **Passkey:** Create a secure wallet using your device’s biometric or passkey authentication.
- **MetaMask:** Connect your existing MetaMask wallet to access the network directly.
- **WalletConnect:** Link any supported wallet app by scanning a QR code.
- **Backup & Recovery:** Restore access to your wallet if you’ve used Incentiv before.

Each topic in the table of contents links to a dedicated guide with step-by-step instructions.
