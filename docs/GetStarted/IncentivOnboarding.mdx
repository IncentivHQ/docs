Welcome to **Incentiv Onboarding**, your complete guide to setting up and accessing the Incentiv ecosystem.

Here, you’ll learn how to create or connect a wallet using **Passkey**, **MetaMask**, or **WalletConnect**, whichever fits you

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Connect using Passkey">
    Create a secure, passwordless **Incentiv Smart Wallet** using your device’s built-in security.

    Everything starts at the [Incentiv Portal](portal.incentiv.io)

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Select Passkey">
        On the welcome screen, choose **Passkey** as your preferred method to create or access your Incentiv Smart Wallet.

        This option lets you set up a secure, passwordless wallet tied to your device’s biometric authentication.

        ![Registration One](/docs/images/RegistrationOne.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Choose a Name">
        1. Next, you will be asked how we should call you.
        2. Enter your preferred **display name**, or click **“Decide for me”** to generate one automatically.
        3. Once ready, click **Continue** to proceed.

        ![Registration Two](/docs/images/RegistrationTwo.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Verify with Biometrics">
        1. You will be prompted to create your wallet using your device’s **biometric authentication** or **PIN**.
        2. Tap **“Verify my ID”** and confirm using **Face ID**, **Touch ID**, or your system’s secure method.
        3. Your wallet will be instantly generated and safely stored under your passkey credentials.

        ![Registration Three](/docs/images/RegistrationThree.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 4: You're All Set">
        Your Incentiv Smart Wallet is now active and securely bound to your device.

        You can use it to access Incentiv, participate in activities, and claim rewards with one click.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Tip>
      **Pro Tip:** You just earned a Badge for activating your first Smart Wallet on Incentiv!
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Connect using MetaMask">
    If you already use MetaMask, you can easily connect it to Incentiv.

    Everything starts at the [Incentiv Portal](portal.incentiv.io)

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Choose MetaMask">
        On the welcome screen, click **MetaMask** as your preferred wallet option.

        This option connects your Incentiv account directly to your MetaMask browser extension or mobile app.

        ![Incentiv Metamask Step 1](/docs/images/OnboardingMetamaskA.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Choose a Name">
        1. You will be asked how we should call you.
        2. Enter your preferred **display name**, or click **“Decide for me”** to automatically generate one.
        3. Then click **Continue**.

        ![Incentiv Metamask Step 2](/docs/images/RegistrationTwo.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Approve the Connection">
        1. You will see a **progress screen** while the connection request is sent to your MetaMask wallet.
        2. Check your **MetaMask popup or browser extension** and approve the connection request.

        ![Incentiv Metamask Step 3](/docs/images/OnboardingMetamaskC.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 4: Add Incentiv Testnet">
        1. Once connected, MetaMask will prompt you to **Add the Incentiv Testnet**.
        2. Click **Approve** to confirm.

        This ensures your wallet is correctly configured to interact with the Incentiv test environment.

        <img
          src="/images/OnboardingMetamaskD-1.png"
          alt="Incentiv Metamask Step 4"
          className="hidden dark:block"
        />
        <img
          src="/images/OnboardingMetamaskD.png"
          alt="Incentiv Metamask Step 4"
          className="dark:hidden"
        />
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 5: You’re All Set">
        Your MetaMask wallet is now connected to Incentiv.

        You can start interacting with the testnet, exploring dApps, and earning rewards.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Tip>
      **Pro Tip:** If the connection doesn’t appear immediately, open MetaMask manually and check for pending requests under Connected Sites.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Connect using WalletConnect">
    <Warning>
      This is not available in the current version of the Mainnet
    </Warning>
    Use **WalletConnect** if you prefer to link a mobile wallet like Trust Wallet, Rainbow, or MetaMask Mobile.

    Everything starts at the [Incentiv Portal](portal.incentiv.io)

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Start the Connection">
        On the welcome screen, choose **WalletConnect** as your preferred wallet option.

        This allows you to securely connect any mobile wallet that supports the protocol.

        ![Incentiv WalletConnect Step 1](/docs/images/Onboarding_WalletConnect1.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Choose a Name">
        1. Next, you will be asked how we should call you.
        2. Enter your preferred **display name** or click **“Decide for me”** to auto-generate one.
        3. Once ready, click **Continue**.

        ![Incentiv WalletConnect Step 2](/docs/images/RegistrationTwo.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Approve the Connection">
        You will now see a progress screen showing that the system is waiting for your wallet connection.

        Keep this window open while your wallet connects.

        ![Incentiv WalletConnect Step 3](/docs/images/Onboarding_WalletConnect3.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 4: Scan and Connect">
        1. A **QR code** will appear.
        2. Open your preferred mobile wallet (such as **Trust Wallet**, **Rainbow**, or **MetaMask Mobile**), select **WalletConnect**, and scan the code to approve.

        ![Incentiv WalletConnect Step 4](/docs/images/Onboarding_WalletConnect4.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 5: You’re All Set">
        Once you approve the request in your mobile wallet, your Incentiv account will connect instantly.

        You can now start exploring the Incentiv ecosystem and enjoy your rewards journey.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
