### **1. How to Secure Your Boxes**

There are two primary ways to get your hands on a Mystery Box:

- **The "Activity Loop" (Free):** Earn **1 Free Mystery Box** for every **20 gas-cost actions** (Swap, Bridge, Mint, Transfer) you complete. 

  <Warning>
    **Critical Rule:** You **must open your earned box** to restart the 20-transaction counter. If a box stays unopened, your progress toward the next free one freezes.
  </Warning>
- **The "XP Purchase":** Skip the wait and buy a box for **120 XP + Gas**<sup>4</sup>. This is the ultimate "Burn to Earn" mechanic for those looking to hunt jackpots instantly.

### **3. XP Booster Mechanics**

Boosters are the key to leaderboard dominance. They trigger automatically upon opening a box<sup>16</sup>:

- **Auto-Queue:** If you already have a booster active, the new one enters a **Queue** and activates the moment the current one expires<sup>17</sup>.
- **Stacking Intensity:** Boosters come in **4 hour** duration. Use them during "Boosted Events" to maximize your velocity.

### **4. Mystery Box Achievements (Badges)**

Opening boxes earns you one-time XP bonuses through badges:

- **First Mystery Box:** Claim/Open your 1st box (**10 XP**).
- **Pandora's Opener:** Open 10 boxes (**50 XP**).
- **Gacha Addict:** Open 100 boxes (**200 XP**).
- **The Oracle:** Open 500 boxes (**1,000 XP**).
- **Jackpot Hunter:** Win a "Legendary" prize from a box (**500 XP**).
