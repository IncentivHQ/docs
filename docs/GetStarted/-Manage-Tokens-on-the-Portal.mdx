## Manage Tokens on the Portal

The Incentiv Portal allows you to monitor your token balances, review activity, and track rewards directly from your dashboard. All updates are reflected automatically based on your on-chain activity and completed ecosystem actions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Access the Dashboard">
    1. Open **portal.incentiv.io** in your browser.
    2. Connect your wallet (MetaMask or Passkey).
    3. Navigate to the **Dashboard** or **Wallet** section.
  </Step>

  ![Manage Tokens1](/images/ManageTokens1.png)

  <Step title="Step 2: View Your Token Balances">
    1. Locate the **Tokens** panel.
    2. Review your available token balances.
    3. Tap a token to view more details.
  </Step>

  ![Manage Tokens2](/images/ManageTokens2.png)

  <Step title="Step 3: Review Token Activity">
    1. Open a specific token.
    2. Check the **Activity** or **Transaction History** section.
    3. Tap a transaction to view additional details.
  </Step>

  ![Manage Tokens3](/images/ManageTokens3.png)

  <Step title="Step 4: Stay Updated">
    1. Check token updates regularly from your dashboard.
    2. Monitor new activities or earning opportunities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Manage Tokens4](/images/ManageTokens4.png)

<Tip>
Your token balances and activity update automatically after completing eligible actions within the Incentiv ecosystem.
</Tip>
