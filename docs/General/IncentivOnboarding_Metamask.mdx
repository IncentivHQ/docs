## Connect to Incentiv Using MetaMask

Follow this step-by-step guide to connect your existing **MetaMask wallet** to Incentiv.

<Steps>
<Step title="Step 1: Choose MetaMask">

On the welcome screen, click **MetaMask** as your preferred wallet option.  

This option connects your Incentiv account directly to your MetaMask browser extension or mobile app.

![Incentiv Metamask Step 1](/docs/images/OnboardingMetamaskA.jpg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 2: Choose a Name">

1. You will be asked how we should call you.  
2. Enter your preferred **display name**, or click **“Decide for me”** to automatically generate one.  
3. Then click **Continue**.

![Incentiv Metamask Step 2](/docs/images/OnboardingMetamaskB.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 3: Approve the Connection">

1. You will see a **progress screen** while the connection request is sent to your MetaMask wallet.  
2. Check your **MetaMask popup or browser extension** and approve the connection request.

![Incentiv Metamask Step 3](/docs/images/OnboardingMetamaskC.jpg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 4: Add Incentiv Testnet">

1. Once connected, MetaMask will prompt you to **Add the Incentiv Testnet**.  
2. Click **Approve** to confirm.  

This ensures your wallet is correctly configured to interact with the Incentiv test environment.

![Incentiv Metamask Step 4](/docs/images/OnboardingMetamaskD.jpg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 5: You’re All Set">

Your MetaMask wallet is now connected to Incentiv.  

You can start interacting with the testnet, exploring dApps, and earning rewards.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Pro Tip:** If the connection doesn’t appear immediately, open MetaMask manually and check for pending requests under "Connected Sites"
</Tip>
