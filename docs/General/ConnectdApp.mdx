---
title: Connect dApp
---

The **Connect dApp** feature allows you to link your **Incentiv Smart Wallet** with decentralized applications (dApps) such as **Uniswap** through **WalletConnect**.  
Follow the steps below to connect securely and start interacting with dApps.

<Steps>
<Step title="Step 1: Open the dApp You Want to Connect">

1. Go to the dApp you want to use, such as **Uniswap**.  
2. Click the **Connect Wallet** button at the top-right corner of the interface.

![Connect dApp Step 1](/docs/images/ConnectdApp1.jpeg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 2: Choose WalletConnect">

1. When prompted to select a wallet, choose **WalletConnect**.  
2. A **QR code** will appear, keep this page open; you’ll use its **connection link** in the next steps.

![Connect dApp Step 2](/docs/images/ConnectdApp2.jpeg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 3: Open the Incentiv Testnet">

1. Go to the **Incentiv Testnet** page.  
2. On the top bar, click **Connect dApp** to open the **WalletConnect Sessions** panel.

![Connect dApp Step 3](/docs/images/ConnectdApp3.jpeg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 4: Paste the WalletConnect URI">

1. From the dApp page, copy the **WalletConnect link** shown under the QR code.
2.Paste it into the **Add New Session** field in your Incentiv testnet and click **Connect**.

![Connect dApp Step 4](/docs/images/ConnectdApp4.jpeg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 5: Confirm Connection">

Once connected, your session will appear under **Active Sessions**, showing the dApp’s name and connection time.

You’re now ready to use your Incentiv Smart Wallet across the dApp ecosystem.

![Connect dApp Step 5](/docs/images/ConnectdApp5.jpeg)
</Step>
</Steps>
