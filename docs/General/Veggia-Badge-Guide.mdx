---
title: "Veggia Badge Guide"
---

**Veggia** is a game platform integrated into the Incentiv ecosystem. By completing your first interaction on Veggia, you can earn the **Veggia badge** on the Incentiv Portal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Visit Veggia">
    1. Open [**https://veggia.io**](https://veggia.io) in your browser.
    2. Connect your wallet and create your Veggia account.

    ![Veggia1](/images/Veggia1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Open Capsules">
    1. Once your account is created, you will enter the **Recruitment Phase**.
    2. Use the **Kidnapper Gacha** to open capsules.
    3. Basic Capsules can be activated periodically and contain **Veggie NFTs with random rarity**.

    <img
      title="Veggia2"
      className="hidden dark:block"
      src="/images/Veggia2-1.png"
      alt="Veggia2"
    />
    <img
      title="Veggia2"
      className="dark:hidden"
      src="/images/Veggia2.png"
      alt="Veggia2"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Complete Your First Interaction">
    1. Open a capsule and receive a Veggie NFT.
    2. This action counts as your **first interaction on Veggia**.

    <img
      title="Veggia3"
      className="hidden dark:block"
      src="/images/Veggia3-1.png"
      alt="Veggia3"
    />
    <img
      title="Veggia3"
      className="dark:hidden"
      src="/images/Veggia3.png"
      alt="Veggia3"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Receive Your Veggia Badge">
    1. Once the interaction is completed, it will be recognized by the Incentiv ecosystem.
    2. You will receive the **Veggia badge** on the Incentiv Portal.

    <img
      src="/images/Veggia4-2.png"
      alt="Veggia4"
      className="hidden dark:block"
    />
    <img
      src="/images/Veggia4-1.png"
      alt="Veggia4"
      className="dark:hidden"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Make sure you are using the same wallet connected to the Incentiv Portal so your Veggia activity can be properly recognized.
</Tip>
