---
title: "MyActivity"
---

The MyActivity section allows you to view and track all your Incentiv transactions and activities in one place. You can inspect each activity in detail to see transaction history, status, and other relevant information.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Click the MyActivity Tab">
    - Click the **MyActivity** tab on the sidebar to open the section.

    ![MyActivity1](/MyActivity1.PNG)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Select and View an Activity">
    - All your activities will be displayed in a list.
    - Click on any activity from the list to view its details.

    ![MyActivity2](/MyActivity2.PNG)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: View Transaction Details">
    - The activity page shows all transaction details, including status, amount, date, and associated wallets.

    ![MyActivity3](/MyActivity3.PNG)
  </Step>
</Steps>
