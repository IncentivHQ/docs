title: "Incentiv Studio"
---

The **Incentiv Studio** is your gateway to the decentralized application layer of the Incentiv ecosystem. It serves as a unified portal for discovering, launching, and building dApps, offering both curated experiences and no-code creation tools. Whether you're a user exploring applications or a builder deploying new ideas, the Arena provides everything you need, intuitively and seamlessly.

### How It Works

<Steps>

<Step title="Step 1: Enter the Studio">
Open the **Incentiv Arena** via the sidebar on the main Incentiv Portal. This takes you to the dApp hub where all activity begins.

![Incentiv Studio](/docs/images/Studio1.jpg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 2: Browse Applications">
Explore featured and community-created dApps. You can search, filter by category, and preview each dApp's description, reviews, and metadata.

![Incentiv Studio](/docs/images/Studio1.jpg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 3: Discover Featured Apps">
At the top of the Arena are promoted dApps, highlighted for their popularity, quality, or relevance. You can launch them directly from the carousel.

![Incentiv Studio](/docs/images/Studio3.jpg)

![Incentiv Studio](/docs/images/Studio4.jpg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 4: Use dApps Seamlessly">
Clicking on a dApp opens its interface within the Arena. All dApps are compatible with Incentiv's smart wallet features, including gas abstraction and unified tokens.

![Incentiv Studio](/docs/images/Studio5.jpg)
</Step>

</Steps>
