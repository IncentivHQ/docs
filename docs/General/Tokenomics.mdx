## \$CENT Token Overview

The **\$CENT token** powers the Incentiv network, rewarding contribution and participation across miners, developers, bundlers, liquidity providers, and users, with a **total supply of 100,000,000,000 \$CENT**.

## Token Allocation

![Tokenomics](/docs/images/Tokenomics.jpg)

### Community Rewards – 26,000,000,000 (26%)

Allocated to incentivize sustained contribution from the network. Rewards are distributed through the Incentiv+ Engine across epochs and vest linearly over 60 months. Supports miners, developers, bundlers, liquidity providers, and active users.

### Short-Term Growth Fund (STGF) – 17,000,000,000 (17%)

Reserved for operational support and early ecosystem acceleration. This includes partnerships, developer grants, liquidity programs, user-growth initiatives, and core operational expenses. Vesting occurs linearly over 24 months.

### Long-Term Growth Fund (LTGF) – 20,000,000,000 (20%)

Holds tokens for long-horizon initiatives that compound network value. Vesting occurs linearly over 60 months.

### Sale – 22,000,000,000 (22%)

Tokens allocated for sale follow a structured release: 3.5% at TGE, 12.5% over the first 12 months, and the remaining 6% from months 13–24.

### Team & Advisors – 15,000,000,000 (15%)

Reserved for contributors and protocol builders. Tokens are locked for 12 months, followed by linear vesting over 24 months to ensure long-term alignment.

  <Step title="Genesis Phase – Bootstrapping Growth">
