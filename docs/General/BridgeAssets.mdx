# Bridge and Send to Another Receiving Wallet

With **Incentiv**, you can easily move your tokens from one wallet to another, even across networks. Follow these simple steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Connect Wallet">
    - Open your Incentiv app or web dashboard.
    - Connect your wallet using your preferred method (built-in wallet, Metamask, WalletConnect, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Select Tokens to Bridge">
    - Choose the token you want to send.
    - Select the network you want to bridge from and to.

    ![Bridge Assets](/bridgeassets1.jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Enter Amount">
    - Input the number of tokens you want to bridge.
    - Review the estimated fees and projected arrival amount.

    ![Bridge Assets](/bridgeassets2.jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Click Bridge">
    - Confirm the transaction by clicking **“Bridge”**.
    - Follow any wallet prompts to authorize the transaction.

    ![Bridge Assets](/bridgeassets3.jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3 (Repeat): Enter Amount">
    - Double-check the amount on the receiving network or wallet.
    - Ensure you account for fees and minimum bridge requirements.

    ![Bridge Assets](/bridgeassets4.jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4 (Repeat): Click Bridge">
    - Confirm again if prompted.
    - Wait for the transaction to complete and verify the tokens in the destination wallet.

    ![Bridge Assets](/bridgeassets5.jpg)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Remember to verify the destination wallet address and network before confirming the bridge.
</Tip>
