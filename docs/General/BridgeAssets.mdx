title: "Bridge Assets"

---

With _Incentiv_, you can easily bridge tokens between wallets or across networks.

<Tip>
  Transfers on Incentiv include gas and interchain fees, and they can take around one minute to complete. You can move funds internally or send them outside the Incentiv ecosystem depending on your destination.
</Tip>


<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Bridge Assets to Connected Wallet">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Connect Wallet">
        - Open the _Incentiv Portal_.
        - Connect your wallet using one of the following: Incentiv Wallet, MetaMask, or WalletConnect.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssets.jpeg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Select Tokens to Bridge">
        - Choose the token you want to bridge.
        - Select the source network and destination network.
        - Choose whether to transfer funds within Incentiv or outside to another network.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsA.jpeg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Enter Amount">
        - Input the amount you want to bridge.
        - Review estimated charges, including _gas fees_ and any applicable _interchain fees_.
        - Check the projected arrival amount.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 4: Click Bridge">
        - Click _“Bridge”_ to confirm.
        - Approve the transaction in your wallet.
        - Bridging typically completes in _around one minute_, depending on network activity.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsC.jpeg)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Tip>
      Always double-check the destination network and wallet before bridging.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bridge Assets to Another Wallet">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Connect Wallet">
        - Description: Open the _Incentiv Portal_ and connect your wallet using one of the following: Incentiv Wallet, MetaMask, or WalletConnect.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssets.jpeg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Enter Another Address">
        - Click the “Receive to Another Address.”
        - Enter the wallet address where you want to receive the bridged assets.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsB.jpeg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Select Tokens to Bridge">
        - Choose the token and select the source and destination networks.
        - Choose whether to transfer funds within Incentiv (internal transfer) or outside to another wallet.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsA.jpeg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 4: Enter Amount">
        - Input the amount to bridge.
        - Review _gas fees_ and _interchain fees_.
        - View the estimated arrival amount.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 5: Click Bridge">
        - Confirm by clicking _“Bridge”_.
        - Approve via your wallet.
        - Bridging usually completes in _about one minute_.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsC.jpeg)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
