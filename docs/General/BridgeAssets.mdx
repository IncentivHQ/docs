<Tab title="Bridge Assets to Another Wallet">
  <Steps>

    <Step title="Step 1">
      Connect your wallet using **MetaMask**, **Incentiv Wallet**, or **WalletConnect**.

      ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssets.jpeg)

      **Optional:** Click **"Receive to Another Address"** if you want to bridge to a different wallet.

      ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssets.jpeg)
    </Step>

    <Step title="Step 2">
      Select the token you want to bridge, then choose the source and destination networks.

      ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsA.jpeg)
    </Step>

    <Step title="Step 3">
      Enter the amount you want to bridge.  
      Review estimated **gas fees**, **interchain fees**, and projected arrival amount.

      ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsB.jpeg)
    </Step>

    <Step title="Step 4">
      Click **"Bridge"** to confirm the transaction.  
      Approve the request in your wallet.  
      Bridging typically completes in **about one minute**.

      ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsC.jpeg)
    </Step>

  </Steps>
</Tab>
