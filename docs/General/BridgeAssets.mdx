With **Incentiv**, you can easily bridge tokens between wallets or across networks. Follow the steps below:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Bridge Assets to Connected Wallet">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Connect Wallet">
        - Open your **Incentiv Portal** or web dashboard.
        - Connect your wallet using any of the supported onboarding methods:
          **Incentiv Wallet**, **MetaMask**, or **WalletConnect**.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssets.jpeg)
      </Step>

      <Step title="Step 2: Select Tokens to Bridge">
        - Choose the token you want to bridge.
        - Select the source network and destination network.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsA.jpeg)
      </Step>

      <Step title="Step 3: Enter Amount">
        - Input the amount you want to bridge.
        - Review estimated charges, including **gas fees** and any applicable **interchain fees**.
        - Check the projected arrival amount.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Step 4: Click Bridge">
        - Click **“Bridge”** to confirm the transaction.
        - Approve the operation in your wallet when prompted.
        - Bridging typically completes in **around one minute**, depending on network activity.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsC.jpeg)
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Always verify the destination network and wallet address before bridging.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Bridge Assets to Another Wallet">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Connect Wallet">
        - Open your **Incentiv Portal** or web dashboard.
        - Connect your wallet using:
          **Incentiv Wallet**, **MetaMask**, or **WalletConnect**.

        **Optional:** Click **“Receive to Another Address”** if bridging to a different wallet.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssets.jpeg)
      </Step>

      <Step title="Step 2: Select Tokens to Bridge">
        - Choose the token you want to bridge.
        - Select the source and destination networks.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsA.jpeg)
      </Step>

      <Step title="Step 3: Enter Amount">
        - Input the amount you want to bridge.
        - Review estimated **gas fees** and **interchain fees**.
        - Check the projected arrival amount.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsB.jpeg)
      </Step>

      <Step title="Step 4: Click Bridge">
        - Confirm by clicking **“Bridge”**.
        - Approve the request in your wallet.
        - Bridging usually completes in **about one minute**.

        ![Bridge Assets](/docs/images/BridgeAssetsC.jpeg)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
