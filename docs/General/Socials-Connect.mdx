---
title: "Socials Connect"
---

The **Social Badge** rewards users for connecting with Incentiv's official social channels. Earn these badges by interacting with Incentiv on X and/or Discord and verifying your action through account connection. Each badge can be earned independently.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="X Connected Badge">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Click Follow on X in the Badge Section">
        - Click the button below the locked X Connected Badge.

        ![SocialX](/docs/images/SocialX1.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Click Connect and Follow @Incentiv_Net on X">
        - Once you click the **Connect button** you will be redirected to X to follow the account.

        ![SocialX](/docs/images/SocialX2.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Go Back to the Portal and Click Verify">
        - Return to the portal to verify the completed action.

        ![SocialX](/docs/images/SocialX5.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 4: Authorize Incentiv Portal on X">
        - Once verified, the X Connected Badge is unlocked and awarded to you.

        ![SocialX](/docs/images/SocialX3.jpg)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 5: You've Successfully Connected your X on Incentiv">
        ![SocialX](/docs/images/SocialX4.jpg)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
