---
title: "Papa Badge"
---

The **Papa Badge** is earned by completing any onchain transaction on [PapaPlay](https://www.papaplay.io/) using the Incentiv network. This guide walks you through the exact manual steps required to connect your wallet and complete a qualifying transaction.

## Unlocking the Papa Badge

To earn the Papa Badge, you must:

- Manually connect your wallet to PapaPlay
- Select the **Incentiv** network
- Complete at least one successful transaction

## How to Access PapaPlay

You can open PapaPlay in either of the following ways:

1. Go to the Incentiv portal and locate the badge labeled **PAPA**.
2. Click the **?** icon above the badge, then **“Complete a transaction on PapaPlay”** will appear.
3. From the popup, click PapaPlay, or go directly to:\
   https://www.papaplay.io/

## How the Papa Badge Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open PapaPlay">
    Navigate to PapaPlay from the Incentiv portal or by visiting the PapaPlay website directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manually Connect Your Wallet">
    Once on PapaPlay:

    - Click **Connect Wallet**
    - Select **Network**
    - Choose **Incentiv**
    - Approve **Connect to dApp** in the wallet popup
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review PapaPlay Reminders">
    After approving the connection, PapaPlay reminders will appear.

    - Read the reminders
    - Click **Get Started**

    <Note>
      Your wallet is only connected after completing these steps.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play a Game and Complete a Transaction">
    After your wallet is connected, choose any game below and complete a transaction.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Lucky Double">
        Place a bet and complete a Lucky Double round.\
        Once the transaction is confirmed onchain, it will count toward the Papa Badge.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Lottie">
        Enter a Lottie game and submit a transaction to participate.\
        Any successful onchain interaction qualifies.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Coin Flip">
        Play a Coin Flip round and confirm the transaction onchain.\
        This is one of the fastest ways to unlock the badge.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
    <Check>
      Any successful transaction qualifies. Once the transaction is confirmed onchain, the Papa Badge will be awarded in the Incentiv portal.
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive the Papa Badge">
    After confirmation, return to the Incentiv portal to see your Papa Badge unlocked.
  </Step>
</Steps>
