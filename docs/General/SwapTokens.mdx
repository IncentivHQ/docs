Token swaps on Incentiv are executed through **smart wallets** using **Account Abstraction**. This enables seamless, gas-optimized trades directly within the Incentiv Portal. Swaps are securely routed and bundled via the alt mempool, ensuring efficient execution and confirmation on-chain.

## Badge Tiers

Each successful swap earns you **Bonus XP (15%)** and progresses your **Swap Badge Tier**. The more swaps you complete, the higher your tier and rewards.

<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center" }}>

| Tier | Name | Requirement | Bonus XP (15%) |
|------|------|------------|----------------|
| T1 | Wood | 1 swap | 3 |
| T2 | Stone | 25 swaps | 75 |
| T3 | Bronze | 75 swaps | 225 |
| T4 | Silver | 250 swaps | 750 |
| T5 | Gold | 750 swaps | 2,250 |

</div>

<Note>XP is automatically credited after transaction confirmation.</Note>

Token swaps are executed through the built-in **Incentiv DEX**. Users can seamlessly exchange supported assets within the portal, with all transactions handled via smart wallets for security and gas efficiency.
