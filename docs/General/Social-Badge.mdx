The Social Badge rewards users for connecting with Incentivâ€™s official social channels. Earn these badges by interacting with Incentiv on X and/or Discord and verifying your action through account connection. Each badge can be earned independently.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="X Connected Badge">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Click the Follow Button">
        - Click the button below the locked X Connected Badge. Click Connect.

        ![SocialX1](/SocialX1.PNG)

        - You will be redirected to the X app to follow Incentiv.

        ![SocialX2](/SocialDiscord2.PNG)

        - Return to the Incentiv portal after following.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Connect Your X Account">
        - Connect your X account in the portal to verify your follow.

        ![SocialX3](/SocialX3.PNG)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Verification Complete">
        - Once verified, the X Connected Badge is unlocked and awarded to you.

        ![SocialX4](/SocialX4.PNG)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Discord Connected Badge">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Click the Join Button">
        - Click the button below the locked Discord Connected Badge.

        ![SocialDiscord1](/SocialDiscord1.PNG)

        - You will be redirected to the Discord app to join the Incentiv server.

        ![SocialDiscord2](/SocialDiscord2.PNG)

        - Return to the Incentiv portal after joining.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Connect Your Discord Account">
        - Connect your Discord account in the portal to verify your membership.

        ![SocialDiscord3](/SocialDiscord3.PNG)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Verification Complete">
        - Once verified, the Discord Connected Badge is unlocked and awarded to you.

        ![SocialDiscord4](/SocialDiscord4.PNG)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
