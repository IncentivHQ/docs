title: "Social Badge"
---

The Social Badge rewards users for connecting with Incentivâ€™s official social channels. Earn these badges by interacting with Incentiv on X and/or Discord and verifying your action through account connection. Each badge can be earned independently.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="X Connected Badge">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Click the Follow Button">
        - Click the button below the locked X Connected Badge. Click Connect. <img src="/docs/images/SocialX1.PNG" alt="SocialX1" />
        - You will be redirected to the X app to follow Incentiv. <img src="/docs/images/SocialX2.PNG" alt="SocialX2" />
        - Return to the Incentiv portal after following.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Connect Your X Account">
        - Connect your X account in the portal to verify your follow. <img src="/docs/images/SocialX3.PNG" alt="SocialX3" />
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Verification Complete">
        - Once verified, the X Connected Badge is unlocked and awarded to you. <img src="/docs/images/SocialX4.PNG" alt="SocialX4" />
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Discord Connected Badge">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1: Click the Join Button">
        - Click the button below the locked Discord Connected Badge. <img src="/docs/images/SocialDiscord1.PNG" alt="SocialDiscord1" />
        - You will be redirected to the Discord app to join the Incentiv server. <img src="/docs/images/SocialDiscord2.PNG" alt="SocialDiscord2" />
        - Return to the Incentiv portal after joining.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2: Connect Your Discord Account">
        - Connect your Discord account in the portal to verify your membership. <img src="/docs/images/SocialDiscord3.PNG" alt="SocialDiscord3" />
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3: Verification Complete">
        - Once verified, the Discord Connected Badge is unlocked and awarded to you. <img src="/docs/images/SocialDiscord4.PNG" alt="SocialDiscord4" />
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
