---
title: "Referral"
---

The Referral system rewards you for bringing new users into Incentiv and helping them complete onboarding. Successful referrals earn XP and unlock powerful XP multipliers.

## Unlocking Referrals

### Step 1: Earn Required Badges

You must already hold:

- X (Twitter) Connect Badge
- Discord Connect Badge
- Faucet Badge
- Send or Swap Badge

## How Referrals Work

### Step 1: Share Your Referral Code

Invite others using your unique referral link.

### Step 2: Referral Onboarding

Referred users must:

- Connect X
- Connect Discord
- Use the Faucet
- Perform a Send or Swap Only fully completed onboarding counts.

### Step 3: Track Progress

Your referral dashboard shows progress, milestones, and multipliers.

## Referral Rewards

### Step 1: Earn XP

Each completed referral grants XP.

### Step 2: Unlock Milestones

Higher referral counts unlock stronger XP multipliers.

### Step 3: Boost All Future XP

Referral multipliers apply to every XP source, not just referrals.
