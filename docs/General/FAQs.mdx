We have compiled answers to common questions to help you navigate the Incentiv ecosystem more confidently.

## General

<Accordion title="What is Incentiv?"> Incentiv is a Layer 1 blockchain designed for simplicity, accessibility, and rewards. It bridges the gap between Web2 and Web3, enabling both technical and non-technical users to interact seamlessly with decentralized applications (dApps). </Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Incentiv custodial?"> No. Incentiv is non-custodial. Users retain full control of their assets through smart contract accounts governed by onchain validation logic. </Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can Incentiv access my funds?"> No. Incentiv cannot unilaterally access or move user funds. All actions require valid onchain authorization from your smart account. </Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are transactions reversible?"> No. Blockchain transactions are final once confirmed. Incentiv cannot reverse or modify completed transactions. </Accordion>

## Transactions and Fees

<Accordion title="Do I need native tokens to pay for gas?"> Not always. Incentiv supports gas abstraction, allowing transaction fees to be sponsored or paid through alternative mechanisms depending on the action being performed. </Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does Incentiv address high gas fees and transaction costs?"> Incentiv reduces gas fees and transaction complexity through an efficient transaction model that allows users to pay fees using the same token being transferred. This simplifies transactions, improves user experience, and makes blockchain interactions more affordable. </Accordion>

## Account Recovery

<Accordion title="What happens if I lose access to my device?"> You can recover your account using the recovery methods configured during onboarding. Refer to Backup and Recovery for supported recovery options. </Accordion>

## Privacy and Security

<Accordion title="How does Incentiv enhance data privacy?"> Incentiv integrates privacy-focused solutions that allow users to control which transactions and balances are publicly visible, reducing unnecessary exposure while maintaining transparency where required for governance. </Accordion>

## Community and Governance

<Accordion title="What role does the community play in Incentiv’s development and governance?"> The Incentiv community contributes through feedback, proposals, and participation in initiatives such as builder grants, hackathons, and meetups that help shape the ecosystem. </Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are Incentiv’s long-term sustainability strategies?"> Incentiv’s sustainability strategy includes adaptive tokenomics, governance-driven treasury management, and ecosystem incentives designed to support long-term network health and adoption. </Accordion>
