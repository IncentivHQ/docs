---
title: "OG Badge Guide"
---

The **OG Badge** is awarded to users who perform their first onchain transaction within the Incentiv ecosystem. It recognizes active participation and engagement with Incentiv's features. Any confirmed transaction inside the platform counts toward unlocking this badge.

## Unlocking the OG Badge

To earn the OG Badge, you must:

- Use the Incentiv portal
- Complete at least one successful onchain transaction

Any confirmed transaction inside Incentiv qualifies.

## How the OG Badge Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Incentiv Portal">
    Go to the Incentiv portal

    - [Swap Tokens](https://docs.incentiv.net/docs/General/SwapTokens)
    - [Bridge Assets](https://docs.incentiv.io/docs/General/BridgeAssets)
