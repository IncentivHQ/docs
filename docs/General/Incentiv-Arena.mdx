title: "Incentiv Arena"
---

The Incentiv Arena is the unified hub for discovering, interacting with, and building decentralized applications (dApps) within the Incentiv ecosystem. It provides a seamless user experience for exploring live dApps and enables no-code deployment via the Incentiv Studio.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Access the Incentiv Arena">
    - Navigate to the **Arena** tab from the left sidebar to open the dApp hub.
    - This is your entry point into the Incentiv ecosystem's app layer.

    ![IncentivArena1](/docs/images/IncentivArena1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Explore Featured dApps">
    - Browse a list of curated dApps available on Incentiv.
    - Each dApp card includes metadata such as name, creator, and usage stats.

    ![IncentivArena2](/docs/images/IncentivArena2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Launch or Interact with a dApp">
    - Click on any dApp card to open it in the Incentiv Arena interface.
    - Interact with the dApp using your Incentiv wallet, benefiting from features like gas abstraction and unified token support.

    ![IncentivArena3](/docs/images/IncentivArena3.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Create a New dApp (No-Code)">
    - To launch your own application, click on the **Create dApp** button.
    - Use the Incentiv Studioâ€™s drag-and-drop builder and logic templates to configure and deploy without writing code.

    ![IncentivArena4](/docs/images/IncentivArena4.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
