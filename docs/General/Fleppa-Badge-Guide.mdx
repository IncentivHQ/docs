## Fleppa Badge Guide

**Fleppa** is a gaming platform integrated into the Incentiv ecosystem. By interacting with Fleppa and completing a game, you can earn the **Fleppa badge** on the Incentiv Portal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Visit Fleppa">
    1. Open **https://fleppa.io** in your browser.
    2. Connect your wallet if prompted.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 2: Choose a Game">
    1. Browse the available games on Fleppa.
    2. Select one of the available games to start playing:
       - **SuperCenti**: Fly Centi through as many pipe gaps as possible without crashing.
       - **SuperX**: Battle in a space arena and survive against other players.
       - **Eat and Run**: Run, collect items, grow stronger, and survive while avoiding elimination.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 3: Play the Game">
    1. Ensure you have **enough funds** in your wallet, as all games require **USDC to start playing**.
    2. Start the selected game.
    3. Complete the game interaction.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 4: Receive Your Fleppa Badge">
    1. After completing a game interaction, the activity will be recognized by the Incentiv ecosystem.
    2. You will receive the **Fleppa badge** on the Incentiv Portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Make sure you are using the same wallet connected to the Incentiv Portal so your Fleppa activity can be properly recognized.
</Tip>
