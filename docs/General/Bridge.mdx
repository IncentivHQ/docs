With _Incentiv_, you can seamlessly move assets **into** or **out of** the Incentiv network across supported chains. Use the tabs below to follow the correct flow based on your direction.

<Tabs>

<Tab title="Bridge In">
<Steps>

<Step title="Step 1: Open Bridge & Connect Wallet">
- Open the **Incentiv Portal**.
- Click **Bridge Assets** in the top navigation.
- Connect your external wallet (e.g. MetaMask).

![Bridge](/docs/images/BridgeIn.jpg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 2: Select Token">
- Click the token selector.
- Choose the asset you want to bridge **into Incentiv**.
- Tokens are grouped by network for easier discovery.

![Token Selection](/docs/images/incentiv-bridge-assets/F67BB2F5-21D8-480A-AA19-217A5FE7878D.jpeg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 3: Configure Transfer">
- Select the **source network** (external chain).
- Select **Incentiv** as the destination network.
- Enter the amount to bridge.
- Optional: toggle **Receive to Another Address** if needed.

![Bridge Config](/docs/images/incentiv-bridge-assets/8443A3A2-8C2A-4651-977F-EF60EB92FEB3.jpeg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 4: Confirm Transaction in Wallet">
- Click **Bridge**.
- Your wallet will prompt a transaction request.
- Review network, amount, and fees carefully.
- Confirm the transaction.

![Wallet Confirm](/docs/images/incentiv-bridge-assets/F3226DDD-092E-4E93-870D-F4A97A7B043D.jpeg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 5: Bridge Completion">
- The bridge transaction is submitted.
- Youâ€™ll see a success notification once confirmed.
- Explorer links are provided for tracking.

![Success Notice](/docs/images/incentiv-bridge-assets/E747238B-7BF9-49B2-B635-2F2F14172AF4.jpeg)
</Step>

</Steps>

<Tip>
Bridging typically completes within a few seconds to a few minutes, depending on network conditions.
</Tip>
</Tab>

<Tab title="Bridge Out">
<Steps>

<Step title="Step 1: Open Bridge">
- Go to the **Incentiv Portal**.
- Click **Bridge Assets** in the upper right.

![Bridge Button](/docs/images/incentiv-bridge-assets/E452D861-5015-4101-89C5-BA23DDAA5AFF.jpeg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 2: Select Token">
- Click on the token selector.
- Choose the asset you wish to bridge **out of Incentiv** to another network.

![Token Picker](/docs/images/incentiv-bridge-assets/62833C65-539A-4AC5-A4DF-EE0BE6D53F26.jpeg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 3: Configure Destination">
- Set **Incentiv** as the source.
- Choose the destination network (e.g. Ethereum).
- Input the amount to bridge.
- (Optional) Toggle _Receive to Another Address_ for external wallets.

![Bridge Form](/docs/images/incentiv-bridge-assets/62357EEF-1AC7-488C-8BB0-B4F39108D5DD.jpeg)
</Step>

<Step title="Step 4: Confirmation">
- Click **Bridge** to initiate transfer.
- Wait for the success message and confirm with provided explorer links.

![Bridge Confirmed](/docs/images/incentiv-bridge-assets/B7639F3B-C4B2-44EB-A65D-504537D11870.jpeg)
</Step>

</Steps>

<Note>
Verify that the destination network supports the selected token before bridging out.
</Note>
</Tab>

</Tabs>
